# Introduction
Hello, we are PIE Dev Studios!

We're a group of friends who are passionate about video games and decided to make our own game!
![PIE Dev Studios Group Photo](assets\IRLPhotos\PIE-Dev-Studios-GroupPhoto.jpg)

## Our History
We started in college, working on small game prototypes and game jams, slowly building our skills and growing our team.
Here are some of the previous games we have created.

### Rat's King
Our first game was "Rat's King", a top down action RPG where you play as Billy, the Rat's bastard knight, as he journeys across
the land in search of his destiny. This game taught us the basics of Unity, the general workflow of game development, and most
importantly that game development was possible, even with no experience.

[Check out Rat's King here](https://firstcrow.itch.io/rats-king)
[![Rat's King](public\assets\GameScreenshots\PieDevOlderProjects\RatsKing.png)](https://firstcrow.itch.io/rats-king)

### The Kiwi That Could
Our second game is "The Kiwi That Could". You play as Tui the Kiwi, an avian engineer who pilots an armored mech, in this 2D
action sidescroller. Our team loved this project and we even had an [amazing intro cutscene by Ellowmello](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kk6-tmvVan4). 
Through this project, our team expanded significantly and we learned the importance of scoping your game project to fit within an MVP.

[Check out The Kiwi That Could here](https://charmiedev.itch.io/the-kiwi-that-could)
[![The Kiwi That Could](public\assets\GameScreenshots\PieDevOlderProjects\TheKiwiThatCould.jpg)](https://charmiedev.itch.io/the-kiwi-that-could)

### Solar Sweets
As we continue to develop games, we decided to publish our projects to Steam! Our first Steam game, Solar Sweets, is a multiplayer baking game, where you 
compete to serve customers by baking different pastries. This was not only our first multiplayer game, but also our first 3D game, so there was a lot
we had to learn while in development. Our largest hurdle with this game was playtesting. Due to how long it took for us to set up online multiplayer,
playtesting took a bit of a backseat. We learned a lot from Solar Sweets and took the lessons from this game to improve our workflows in future projects.

[Check out Solar Sweets here](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3056870/Solar_Sweets/)
[![Solar Sweets](public\assets\GameScreenshots\SolarSweets\SolarSweets_Screenshot1.png)](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3056870/Solar_Sweets/)

# Absolute Momentum
This brings us to now. We are hard at work on our next game, Absolute Momentum, which brings together everything we have learned up until this point.
Absolute Momentum is our first, feature-complete title that will be launching on Steam.
![Absolute Momentum Banner](public\assets\GameScreenshots\AbsoluteMomentum\absmom_banner.jpg)
The game is a multiplayer parkour racing game that you can play with friends online.

Set in a near-future Earth, this game puts you in the driver's seat of a global racing phenomenon. Race as one of our champions including Baku, Etoile, Sangeo, Bia, and Leilei.

Our goal is to deliver a fast paced, action packed racing game for you and your friends to play. 

While we develop this project, we want to bring you along for the ride as well! On our YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram, we'll be posting dev vlogs discussing our
journey developing this game. We will also be uploading blogs to this website, showing off concept art, early builds, and our decisions as we continue to build this game.

We hope you will join us for this journey and we'll see you soon!
